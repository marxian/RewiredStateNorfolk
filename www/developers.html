The theme is “Code a Better County” - a riff on Rewired State's own “Code a Better Country”. It's a hackday focused on data about one county. http://rewiredstate.org/projects and http://linkedgov.hackcamp.org.uk/hacks should give you some idea of the sort of thing that gets produced.

If you think that sounds interesting, please sign up here

We'll be hosting at our new office. We'll open for business at 9.30am with a brief kickoff at 10am, and feed you throughout.

At 6.30pm, we'll gather with some guests to present what's been created over the day. That'll include our sponsors, some of our local government friends, and others who've signed up.
