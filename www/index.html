Rewired State : Norfolk 7th May 2011 - Norwich

Rewired State runs hack days to let developers show government what is possible and government show developers what is needed.

Neontribe is a Norfolk web development company who've been involved with Rewired State for most of its life. Most recently, we were one of four hosting organisations for Young Rewired State 2010, the event that gave birth to the quite marvellous GovSpark.

Rewired State: Norfolk will bring Norfolk data, professional developers and young people together. It's the first of its kind, authorised by Rewired State, organised by NeonTribe. It'll be a day-long a hackfest in Norwich on Saturday May the 7th aimed at producing proofs-of-concept that show what can be done with Norfolk's data. Visualisations, apps, utilities, services...
