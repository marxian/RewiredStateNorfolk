Rewired State: Norfolk will bring Norfolk data, professional developers and young people together. It's the first of its kind, authorised by Rewired State, organised by Neontribe. It'll be a day-long hackfest in Norwich on Saturday May the 7th aimed at producing proofs-of-concept that show what can be done with Norfolk's data. Visualisations, apps, utilities, services…
